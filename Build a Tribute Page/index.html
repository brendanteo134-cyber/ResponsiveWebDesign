When you have eliminated all which is impossible, then whatever remains, however improbable, must be the truth.

-- One of Sherlock Holmes most iconic quotes. Being a key part of deductive reasoning, focusing logic on what is possible. Such logic can easily be applied in scientific inquiry, legal investigations, or any complex problem-solving scenario where you can definitively rule out certain possibilities, narrowing the field to the most likely or improbable remaining explanation.
